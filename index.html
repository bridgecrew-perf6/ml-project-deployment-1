Input Attributes Details
Pregnancy

Glucose
Possible Values of Glucose are :
  • The Glucose level of the Female Patient should range between 70 to 180
BloodPressure
Possible Values of Glucose are :
  • The BloodPressure level of the Patient should range between 0 to 120
SkinThickness
Possible Values of SkinThickness are :
  • The SkinThickness level of the Female Patient should range between 0 to 100
Insulin
Possible Values of Insulin are :
  • The Insulin level of the Female Patient should range between 0 to 1000
Age
Possible Values of Insulin are :
  • The Age of the Female Patient should range between 1 to 100

Step 2 : Click on Predict Diabetes Button
